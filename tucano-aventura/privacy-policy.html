Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Thank you for playing Toucan Adventure (Tucano Aventura). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while playing.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our game is designed to be played for fun without requiring you to create an account or provide any personal information directly to us. We do not collect names, emails, or personal addresses.

2. Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our game. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include Advertising IDs and general location data. To learn more about how Google uses your data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

3. Local Storage

The game saves your progress (such as your High Score) locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers and is only used to enhance your gameplay experience.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal data, we immediately delete this from our servers.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at devoluapp@gmail.com.
